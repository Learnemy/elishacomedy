Singaporean Comedy in the Bay

Elisha Tan is a Singaporean comedian based in San Francisco Bay Area, California, who made history as the first Singaporean to headline the iconic Punch Line San Francisco. Known for her quick wit and infectious energy, Elisha transforms the challenges of navigating a new culture and society’s expectations of women into relatable, hilarious stories.

Elisha's comedy journey has taken her all over the world, including the USA, Singapore, Germany, Ireland, and the UK. She has lit up the stages of world-renowned clubs like the Laugh Factory, Cobb’s Comedy Club, and Comedy Crunch (Dublin) and prestigious festivals like the Detroit Women of Comedy Festival, Loons on the Lake Comedy Festival, and the Golden Laughs Comedy Festival.

When she’s not on stage, Elisha runs Average Foundation, a community fund supporting small social impact projects, and produces the Good Giggles comedy show to raise funds for local non-profits. Approachable, fun, and always up for snacks, Elisha is on a mission to spread positivity through laughter.
